About

VANTIS, also known as Anuj Parashar, is a 21-year-old music producer and a college student from India. Inspired by artists such as Martin Garrix, Alesso, Alan Walker, Marshmello, and Illenium, he creates music that blends emotion, melody, and energy across genres including Progressive House, Future Bass, Future House, Trap, and Hip-Hop. Having spent the last four years learning, experimenting, and refining his craft, VANTIS is constantly exploring new sounds and creative directions. His approach to music is driven by curiosity and a passion for growth, always looking to learn something new and push beyond familiar boundaries. As an artist, he believes the journey of creating music never truly ends, and each track is an opportunity to evolve, connect, and express something meaningful.
